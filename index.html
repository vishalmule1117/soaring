Description

A breathtaking hill station and famous for its sparkling lake & gorgeously glistening waterfall is Bhandardara. It is situated 180 kilometers from Mumbai. Splendorous routes and a close call to nature are what call walking and trekking enthusiasts. It houses the beautiful Amruteshwar Temple of Lord Shiva built in the 11th Century AD. Bhandardara is also renowned as a waterfall destination and the best time to visit this place is the monsoon season.
